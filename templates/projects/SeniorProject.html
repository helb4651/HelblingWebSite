Design

      My favorite part of this project was focusing on the needs of our customer. Instead of jumping quickly to code, we spent significant time ensuring we’d meet the needs of our end users. Our advisor from Hp walked us through a process called design thinking. Design thinking helps to make valuable products by meeting the needs of customers. Our product was much more valuable because of this process and I’ve found design thinking has value in multiple scopes of software design.
